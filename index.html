pig - dice game

Each turn, a player repeatedly rolls the dice until either a 1 is rolled on any of the die or the player decides to "hold":

  • If the player rolls a 1, their CURRENT score gets lost and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If the player rolls any other number, the sum of both the dice is added to their CURRENT score and the player's turn continues.
  • If a player chooses to "hold", their CURRENT score is added to their TOTAL score, and it becomes the next player's turn.

The first player to score 100 or more points on TOTAL score wins
